## Product Blueprint V2 Alignment

Current planning should align with `docs/product-blueprint-v2.md`. Papaipay consists of two products:

1. Corporate Website
2. Asset Participation Platform

Milestones should prioritize Member and Admin flows, eKYC third-party integration readiness, asset opportunities, contributions, payment gateway readiness, and reporting. Advisor workflows, case management, and financial assessment-led onboarding should not be treated as primary roadmap drivers unless explicitly reintroduced.
